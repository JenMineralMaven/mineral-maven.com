4-Week Meal Plan

4-Week High Mineral Busy Mom Meal Plan

Set-and-forget crockpot dinners, mineral-rich breakfasts and snacks, and weekly grocery lists — all designed around your postpartum recovery.

🫕 28 crockpot dinners 🛒 4 weekly grocery lists 🍕 Pizza and burger nights included 🌸 Breastfeeding-safe
How this plan works: Each week follows the same simple rhythm — crockpot on in the morning, dinner ready by evening. Breakfasts and snacks are mineral-dense and require zero cooking. Every week has its own grocery list so you're never buying more than you need.
Week 1
Foundation Week — Iron and Zinc Focus
Monday · Dinner
Slow-Cooked Lamb Shoulder with Root Vegetables
Season lamb shoulder generously with salt, pepper, garlic, and rosemary. Place in crockpot with chopped carrots, parsnip, onion, and 1 cup bone broth. Cook on low 8 hours. Serve over mashed potato. Key minerals: zinc, iron, B12, selenium.
Tuesday · Dinner
Beef and Lentil Stew
Brown 500g beef mince then add to crockpot with 1 cup red lentils, 1 can diced tomato, 2 cups beef broth, diced onion, garlic, cumin. Cook on low 6 hours. Add 2 large handfuls spinach in the last 30 minutes. Key minerals: iron, zinc, magnesium, folate.
Wednesday · Easy Night
Grass-Fed Beef Burgers with Avocado
Pan-fry or grill 150g grass-fed beef patties. Serve on sourdough with avocado, lettuce, tomato, and a fried egg on top. Key minerals: zinc, iron, choline, magnesium.
Thursday · Dinner
Chicken Thigh and Sweet Potato Curry
Add bone-in chicken thighs, 1 can coconut milk, 2 tbsp curry powder, 1 large diced sweet potato, 1 can chickpeas, garlic, ginger to crockpot. Cook 6–7 hours on low. Stir through spinach before serving over rice. Key minerals: zinc, iodine, selenium, vitamin A.
Friday · Easy Night
Homemade Pizza Night
Use store-bought base. Top with tomato, mozzarella, beef mince, spinach, and pumpkin seeds. The pumpkin seeds are non-negotiable — they are one of the richest food sources of zinc and magnesium. Key minerals: zinc, magnesium, iron.
Saturday · Dinner
Salmon and Vegetable Chowder
Add diced potato, corn, leek, garlic, fish stock, and cream to crockpot. Cook on low 5 hours. Add salmon fillets in the last 45 minutes. Season with dill and lemon. Key minerals: DHA, selenium, iodine.
Sunday · Prep Day
Bone Broth + Roast Chicken
Roast a whole chicken for dinner. Keep the carcass. Before bed, add carcass to crockpot with water, apple cider vinegar, onion, celery, carrot. Cook overnight on low 10–12 hours. Strain and use all week in soups, sauces, or drink warm. Key minerals: glycine, calcium, phosphorus, trace minerals.
Week 2
Magnesium and DHA Focus
Monday · Dinner
Slow-Cooker Beef Short Ribs
Season ribs with salt, smoked paprika, garlic powder. Add to crockpot with 1 cup beef broth, onion, and 2 tbsp tomato paste. Cook on low 8 hours. Serve with mashed sweet potato and steamed broccoli. Key minerals: iron, zinc, B12, collagen.
Tuesday · Dinner
Turkish-Style Lamb and Chickpea Stew
Diced lamb shoulder, 1 can chickpeas, 1 can diced tomato, cumin, coriander, cinnamon, garlic, onion. Cook on low 7 hours. Serve with quinoa and a dollop of full-fat yoghurt. Key minerals: zinc, iron, magnesium, selenium.
Wednesday · Easy Night
Sardines on Sourdough Toast
This one sounds humble but it is a mineral powerhouse. Sardines on thick sourdough with avocado, sliced tomato, lemon, and cracked pepper. Add a soft-boiled egg on the side. Key minerals: DHA, calcium, selenium, iodine, iron.
Thursday · Dinner
Pork and Apple Slow Cooker
Pork shoulder with sliced apple, onion, wholegrain mustard, apple cider vinegar, garlic, thyme. Cook on low 8 hours. Pull apart and serve over creamy mashed potato with roasted brussels sprouts. Key minerals: zinc, B vitamins, selenium.
Friday · Easy Night
Steak and Eggs
Pan-sear a grass-fed sirloin or rump steak. Serve with 2 fried eggs, sauteed spinach with garlic and lemon, and sourdough. Simple, fast, and one of the most mineral-dense meals you can eat. Key minerals: iron, zinc, B12, choline, selenium.
Saturday · Dinner
Thai-Style Coconut Prawn Soup
Add coconut milk, fish stock, lemongrass, ginger, garlic, fish sauce, lime, and mushrooms to crockpot. Cook 4 hours on low. Add prawns in the last 20 minutes. Serve over rice noodles with fresh coriander. Key minerals: iodine, zinc, selenium, DHA.
Sunday · Slow Cook
Whole Roast Chicken in Crockpot
Rub whole chicken with butter, salt, paprika, garlic. Place breast-side up in crockpot on a bed of sliced onion and lemon. Cook on low 7–8 hours. The skin won't crisp but the meat is incredibly tender. Save carcass for broth. Key minerals: zinc, B6, niacin, selenium.
